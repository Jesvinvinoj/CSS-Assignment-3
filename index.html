Toblerone: The Swiss Chocolate Delight

Toblerone is a delightful Swiss chocolate recognized for its distinctive triangular shape and honey-almond nougat.

Interesting Facts

  • Established in 1908 by Theodor Tobler in Bern, Switzerland.
  • The iconic triangular shape draws inspiration from the Swiss Alps.
  • The original Toblerone recipe includes milk chocolate, honey, and almond nougat.
